01 — Our Story

A laboratory rooted in Vietnam,
focused on the world.

DO.SON was founded with a singular vision — to merge pharmaceutical research with refined skincare formulation. Inspired by Vietnam's botanical heritage and disciplined by modern dermatological science, we develop products that deliver both measurable efficacy and sensorial elegance.

We believe true luxury lies not in excess but in precision. Every formulation is designed around ingredient synergy, skin-barrier science, and long-term resilience — never quick fixes.

04 — Our approach

Where laboratory rigour
meets botanical poetry.

I

Discovery in the field

Vietnam's biodiversity offers a rich palette of botanicals — lotus, mangosteen, green tea, fermented rice. We study them not as folklore but as candidate actives, characterised molecule by molecule.

  • Botanical sourcing
  • Phytochemical analysis
  • Sustainable harvest
II

Stabilised by chemistry

Active raw materials are fragile. We engineer delivery systems — encapsulation, fermentation, buffering — so each formula remains potent from manufacture to last application.

  • Encapsulation
  • Fermentation
  • pH engineering
III

Validated on the skin

Every candidate is tested against barrier, irritation, and tolerance markers before progressing — efficacy is never assumed, only demonstrated.

  • Barrier markers
  • Tolerance testing
  • Sensorial review

05 — Our research process

From discovery to dermatology,
in disciplined steps.

  1. 01

    Botanical sourcing

    Vietnamese botanicals are selected and characterised for activity, purity, and reproducibility before entering our pipeline.

  2. 02

    Active stabilisation

    Each candidate active is encapsulated, buffered, or fermented to maximise stability and skin compatibility.

  3. 03

    In-vitro & barrier studies

    Formulations are screened on barrier markers, irritation potential, and synergistic activity before any clinical step.

  4. 04

    Pilot & pharmaceutical-grade scale

    Validated formulas transition to GMP-aligned manufacture with full lot traceability and quality control.
